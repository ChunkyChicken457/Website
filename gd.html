How to Play
SpaceW↑ or click to jump / interact.
Cube: Jump over spikes and blocks.
Ship: Hold to fly up, release to fall. Lands on floor safely.
Ball: Press on surface to flip gravity.
Wave: Hold = fly up diagonally, release = fall diagonally.
Orbs: 🟡 Jump · 🔵 Flip gravity · 🟣 Mini · 🟠 Boost · 👻 Phase
Portals switch your mode. 🅂-blocks are safe for ships.
